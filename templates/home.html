Understanding Blood Cells

Blood cells play a vital role in maintaining human health. Red Blood Cells (RBCs) carry oxygen throughout the body, White Blood Cells (WBCs) help fight infections, and Platelets assist in blood clotting. Accurate identification of blood cell types is important for diagnosing various medical conditions.
